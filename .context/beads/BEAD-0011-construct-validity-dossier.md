## Description

Structured validity argument for each eval suite — content, criterion, construct, external, consequential validity evidence. Answers "does this eval measure what it claims to?"

## Frameworks

- Cronbach-Meehl validity (from old tomography rig design)
- Salaudeen et al. 2025, "Measurement to Meaning" validity framework
- Wallach et al. 2025, social science measurement 4-level framework
- Freiesleben 2026, nomological networks

## Product integration

- Part of the "eval health report" that ships alongside every run
- Provides the structured answer to question 2 ("does the eval's design cast doubt on the result?")
- Could be partially automated (item diagnostics, factor structure, sensitivity profile feed into validity evidence)

## When to revisit

- After IRR, sensitivity, and IRT are integrated (they provide the evidence for the dossier)
- When building the reporting layer
